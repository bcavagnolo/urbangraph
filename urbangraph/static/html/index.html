URBANgraph

URBANgraph is an online tool for visualizing the results of urban simulations from URBANsim. The next sensible question you might have is, "What is URBANsim?" You could just go read about it at that link I just typed. But here's the basics: urban planners gather a bunch of data (like census data and buisiness location data) for a region of interest. Then they use URBANsim to estimate statistical models like OLS models and logit models. They also develop policies like urban growth boundaries, affordable housing subsidies, and restrictions on parking. Multiple polices are grouped together as a scenario. The models can then be used to forcast things, like where people will choose to live, where businesses will choose to locate, and what sorts of things developers will choose to build under the proposed policies. URBANgraph is a tool to visualize the output data as graphs.
